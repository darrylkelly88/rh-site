The Business Solutions Centre is a State of the Art Facility with Mutli-Vendor Technologies & Demonstrations available to Partners and their Customers

What is the Business Solutions Centre?

Based in Bracknell the Business Solutions Centre is a multi-purpose resource facility. It can be used by TDSynnex partners for events, training & enablement, or to deliver engaging demonstrations to your customers.

TD Synnex Partners can expect the following types of Red Hat activities as part of an engagement with the Business Solutions Centre:

RHCE Guidance and Enablement

  • Access to Red Hat Training Material for free
  • One-to-One support to discuss training topics
  • Access to additional material to support study

Demonstrations

  • Support in delivering demonstrations to your customers
  • Support in building out your own demonstrations
  • Access to fun, engaging and interactive demonstrations not available else-where.

Sandpit Environments

  • Support with access to Red Hat NFR Subscriptions
  • Access to Infrastructure to build or learn
  • Support in building out demonstrations
