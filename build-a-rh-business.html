Add additional users to your partner account
20 Minutes

To make sure that everyone can access the content and training that Red Hat make available to partners any additional people will need an account linked to your main partner account. This can be done by following the steps below:

  1. Go to Red Hat Partner Connect
  2. Go to Log in at the top right corner of the page.
  3. Choose ‘Log in for Business Partner’.
  4. Go to ‘Company Info’ on the left side of the page.
  5. Go to ‘User Administration’ on the left side of the page
  6. Click on ‘manage users’

Here you will have the choice to invite a user with an existing Red Hat login (which is not linked to a partnership account) or create a Red Hat login for the user:

  • To create a Red Hat login for the user:
    • Fill in the fields that are required
    • Per email address, users can have more than one login
    • In the last field, you can choose to make the user admin (admins can add users)
    • Click ‘save’
  • To invite a user with an existing Red Hat login:
    • Fill in the fields that are required
    • Make sure you have the corporate user login
    • In the last field, you can choose to make the user admin
    • Click ‘save’
